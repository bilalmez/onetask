Setup Guide

Get OneTask connected to your Notion workspace in 3 steps.

1
Duplicate the Notion Template
🔗
Open the OneTask Notion template and click Duplicate to copy it to your workspace. It includes the database, Board view, and all required columns ready to go.
→ Open Template ↗
⚙️
Prefer manual setup? Create a Notion database with these columns:
• Name — Title • Status — Status • Priority — Select • Notes — Text
Priority options: Week Focus · High · Medium · Low
2
Create & Connect an Integration
🔑
A. Create the integration:
Go to notion.so/my-integrations → New integration → copy the token → paste it as NOTION_TOKEN in your .env file.

B. Connect it to your database:
Open your OneTask page in Notion → click ⋯ (top right) → Connections → search for your integration → confirm.
⚠️ If the integration is not connected under Connections, the app will start but won't find or update your tasks.
3
Find Your Database ID
🔍
Open your database in Notion and look at the URL:
notion.so/abc123def456789...?v=...
The highlighted part is your Database ID. Paste it as NOTION_DATABASE_ID in your .env file.
✅
You're all set! Restart the app after updating your .env file and you're ready to focus.
